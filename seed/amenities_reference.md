## Overlap — Appears in Both Hotel & Room Level

| Amenity | Note |
|---------|------|
| `Free WiFi` | Hotel = property-wide network. Room = in-room access. |
| `Butler Service` | Hotel = available as hotel service. Room = dedicated butler for that room. |
| `Airport Transfer` | Hotel = general shuttle. Room = private transfer included in room rate. |

> These 3 overlaps are intentional — the meaning differs by level.
> Booking.com and Agoda use the same pattern.
